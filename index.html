Alaska Salmon Species

King Salmon
The King Salmon, also known as Chinook Salmon, is the largest species of Pacific salmon. They are prized for their rich flavor and high oil content.
Sockeye Salmon
Sockeye Salmon, also called Red Salmon, are known for their deep red flesh color and robust flavor. They are a favorite for grilling and smoking.
Coho Salmon
Coho Salmon, or Silver Salmon, have a milder flavor compared to other species. They are often used in a variety of culinary dishes.
Pink Salmon
Pink Salmon, also known as Humpback Salmon, are the most abundant species of Pacific salmon. They have a delicate flavor and are often canned.
Chum Salmon
Chum Salmon, also called Dog Salmon, have pale flesh and a lower fat content compared to other species. They are commonly used for canning and smoking.
